A Moveable Feast

Who are we?

Living in big city can make the list of places to eat and drink intimidating. With a 100000 restaurants in Shanghai it impossible to try everything and like many you end up relying on a chosen few. A Moveable Feast hopes to connect curious people with good food, good company and great restaurants. Each month a wide range of dining options will be curated allowing for those new to Shanghai or even an old China hand to find something new, old or simply enjoyable. Members are encouraged to volunteer new places. Be it your local noodle joint, your favourite Korean barbecue or a hidden cocktail bar. Restaurants range from local inexpensive, to further a field in Asia to Europe and maybe the odd Michelin starred restaurant should budgets allow.
